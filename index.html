Application Requirements

Essential documents needed for university admission in China

Passport

A valid passport is required for all international students applying to Chinese universities.

  • Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your intended stay
  • Clear scanned copy of the photo page
  • Previous passports if applicable

Recommendation Letters

Strong recommendation letters enhance your application.

  • 2 letters required for most programs
  • From professors or employers familiar with your work
  • On official letterhead with signature
  • Must be recent (within 6 months)

Language Proficiency

Proof of English or Chinese language proficiency depending on program requirements.

  • TOEFL (minimum 80 iBT)
  • IELTS (minimum 6.0 overall)
  • Chinese HSK4 and above
  • Some universities accept Duolingo English Test

Academic Documents

Official academic records from previous institutions.

  • High school diploma for undergraduate programs
  • Bachelor's degree for graduate programs
  • Official transcripts (translated to English/Chinese)
  • Course descriptions for credit transfer
